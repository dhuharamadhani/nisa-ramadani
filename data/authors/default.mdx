Nisa Ramadani is an artist from Yogyakarta. She graduated from Faculty of Psychology, Universitas Gadjah Mada. Her works mostly focused on performance art and performing art (actor and dancer).

She participated in OpenLab program by Teater Garasi/Garasi Performance Institute and it becomes her starting point to develop career as an artist. She is also part of Proyek Edisi, a young artists' initiative based in Yogyakarta.
