_Title_: What I Think About When I Think About It

_Artist_: Nisa Ramadani

## Short Description

A durational performance art using white strings, presented in Proyek Edisi Head Quarter, RT 01, Dusun Jeblok, Dukuh 3, Tirtonirmolo, Kasihan, Bantul Regency, 55181. This performance art was held on 17 September 2022, from 10.30 am to 18.30 pm (total duration is eight hours).
